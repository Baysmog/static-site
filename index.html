Quick Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a STAR certified shop?

Yes we are STAR certified.

What is the $8.95 CA Certificate Fee?

This fee is paid only if your vehicle passes the Smog test and it goes to the California Bureau of Automotive Repair which regulates the Smog program. The only time this fee does not apply is if the vehicle fails the emission test or it is not being registered in California.

Will I need to send any paperwork in once the Smog is complete?

No, all Smog test results are automatically submitted directly to the DMV, Plus we will provide you a copy for your records.

Does the owner need to be present for the Smog?

No, the owner does not need to be present just someone to authorize the Smog check.

What exactly do I need to bring in for the Smog check?

If the Smog check is for a registration renewal any notice from the DMV is helpful, otherwise just bring the vehicle in and we can take it from there (even vehicles from out of state), but any paperwork is helpful.

Im from Out of State do I need anything prior to the Smog Check?

Nothing is needed in fact you can save yourself a trip by coming here first.

Do I need to have the form that DMV mailed me to get the Smog check, what if I lost it?

No problem, just come in and we will take care of you. However if you do have the form please bring it. It really helps expedite the process.

Do you perform Clean Truck Checks?

Yes we do provide Clean Truck Checks for Diesel Vehicles that weigh more than 14000lbs, please call 510-887-4735 for additional information. Here is a Clean Truck Check Coupon.
